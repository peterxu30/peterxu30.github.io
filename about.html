About Me

I’m Peter Xu, currently a freshman at the University of California, Berkeley intending to major in computer science. This semester I’m currently taking Data Structures (CS 61B), Discrete Math and Probability Theory (CS 70), and Introduction to Cognitive Science (CogSci 1). Besides classes, I’m also a lab assistant for the Structure and Interpretation of Programs (CS 61A).

When not programming, I enjoy running, eating, reading, gaming, and writing. I’m a fan of science fiction and among my favorite books are Rendezvous with Rama and Childhood’s End by Arthur C. Clarke. I play League of Legends in my free time as well as other games; I’m currently working my way through Borderlands 2. I don't watch television often, but I absolutely love Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra.
